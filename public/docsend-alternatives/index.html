Honest DocSend Review and 10 DocSend Alternatives in July 2026

DocSend review and why we built Papermark · Updated July 2026

I used DocSend myself for many years, first for startup fundraising, then for managing a Fund of Funds. So I knew every pain point because I experienced it myself: poor customer support, limited customization, no custom domains, and documents that were vulnerable to third party downloaders.

After Dropbox acquired DocSend in 2021, things did not get better. The product stagnated. The UI had not changed in years. Support got worse. Pricing went up. We knew something had to change.

So we said: let's stop sharing documents like it is 2013.

That is why we built Papermark. It started as a tweet, a fun side project. The first version had no grand plan. But after the launch and thousands of reactions, we felt a strong market pull. Then it was one small improvement after another, step by step, feature by feature.

After 15 years in engineering, I know that the hard part is not the MVP. It is the long road after. And yes, I see many vibe coded alternatives appearing now. But by April 2026 our team has made over 1,500 commits to build the product Papermark is today, talking with every customer we can, analyzing every request, and making sure the foundation is solid and the product is better than ever.

Today, in July 2026, we welcome more than 60,000 companies who have transitioned from DocSend, legacy data rooms, or document sharing solutions that cost between $100 and $10,000 a month.

The most popular DocSend alternatives in July 2026 fall into three categories: Google Drive type setups with limited security and customization, heavy legacy VDR providers like Intralinks or Datasite with custom-quote pricing, and newly built vibe coded data rooms with shallow feature sets.

When you make a decision on alternatives, it usually comes down to a few core needs you need to consider: the security features you actually need, the pricing you want to pay, page by page analytics, data room features, custom branding, and how much storage is included.

If you think of Papermark, we sit in the middle of the market. But we fit a broad range of use cases - from simple document sharing with page by page analytics to advanced features with AES 256 encryption, SOC 2, GDPR, and ISO 27001 certifications. As of July 2026, our Data Rooms plan starts at €99/month with a 7-day free trial.

At the core of Papermark is each customer, each experience, each bug fixed in hours, not weeks. Because when companies trust us with their most sensitive documents - investor decks, M&A materials, legal contracts - we take it seriously.
