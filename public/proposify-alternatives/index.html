Why to Select an Alternative to Proposify?

While Proposify is a well-known platform for sharing sales proposals, there are several compelling reasons to consider alternatives that may better suit your document sharing needs.

1. Expensive Sharing Costs

Recent price increases have made Proposify less accessible for many businesses.

“Used it for a year and then they doubled their prices. Having to drop it at the end of the year.” - User Review

2. Limited Analytics Capabilities

The platform's tracking and analytics features don't provide the depth of insights many businesses need for effective proposal tracking.

3. Technical and Access Issues

Users report significant challenges with document sharing and access.

“Prospects/clients can't always access their proposals. Download functionality breaks frequently. Mobile app is terrible... We're migrating away because it's become yet another tool that someone needs to ‘manage’ instead of it ‘just working’” - User Review

4. Support and Integration Problems

Integration issues and unreliable support have caused frustration.

“Expectations of the tool that were promised to our team have been wildly inaccurate. There are lots of bugs with: the salesforce integration, the fee tables, the workflow.” - User Review
