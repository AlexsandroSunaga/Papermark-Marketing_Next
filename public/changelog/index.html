Multi-Folder Upload Permissions

Control upload permissions for multiple folders at once

Managing visitor upload permissions just got easier with bulk folder controls.

What's new:

  • Select multiple folders — apply upload permissions to several folders at once
  • Bulk permission updates — enable or disable uploads across folder groups
  • Visitor group support — different upload rights for different visitor groups

Download Entire Data Room as Archive

Download complete data rooms as zip files with one click

Need an offline backup or local copy of your data room? You can now download the entire data room as a zip archive.

Key features:

  • One-click download — get all folders and files in a single zip file
  • Preserves structure — folder hierarchy maintained in the archive
  • Large data room support — handles data rooms with hundreds of documents
  • Owner-only access — available to data room owners and admins

Perfect for creating backups before major changes, archiving completed deals, or preparing offline review copies for stakeholders. Find the download option in your data room settings.

April 17, 2026v0.20.7

Freeze Datarooms

Close datarooms permanently and generate downloadable archives with time-limited access links

You can now freeze datarooms to close them permanently while preserving all content and activity.

What freezing does:

  • Blocks all visitor access — No one can view documents or upload files
  • Prevents modifications — Upload, reorder, and indexing are disabled
  • Triggers archive generation — Creates a downloadable ZIP of all dataroom content
  • Provides secure download links — Time-limited URLs for retrieving the archive

Perfect for closing completed deals, archiving old fundraising rounds, or preserving M&A transaction records.

Find the freeze option in your dataroom's Danger Zone settings.

  • Deal role — buyers, sellers, advisors, legal counsel
  • Organization — group all visitors from the same company
  • Access level — tier 1, tier 2, or full access groups
  • Custom criteria — any grouping that fits your workflow

Enterprise SSO with SAML & SCIM
