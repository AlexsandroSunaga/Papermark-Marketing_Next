What are AI agents for documents?

AI agents are persistent workflows that can read, reason over and act on your documents. Instead of manually scanning hundreds of pages, you describe the task once and let the agent handle summarization, extraction and checks every time new material lands in your data room.

Papermark focuses these capabilities on dealmaking, legal work and compliance. Your agents live where your sensitive documents already are, with full access control and analytics.

You choose the role, such as AI lawyer or AI due diligence analyst, connect the relevant folders and let the agent answer questions, surface red flags and generate structured outputs that are easy to share with your team.

How Papermark AI agents work with your data rooms

Papermark combines secure data rooms with an AI document assistant so you can search, summarize and reason over entire folders. AI agents build on this foundation with reusable prompts, workflows and checks tailored to specific jobs.

You decide which folders an agent can access, whether that is a fundraising data room, a bundle of NDAs or a regulatory evidence pack. The agent uses retrieval to pull only relevant passages, runs them through large language models and posts answers with clear citations back into your workspace.

Unlike generic chatbots, Papermark AI agents are tied to link-level permissions, watermarking and page-by-page analytics, so you keep full visibility and control while your team benefits from automation.
