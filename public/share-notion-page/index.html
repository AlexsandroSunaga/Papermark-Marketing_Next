Quick overview: Share a Notion page with Papermark

  1. Create a Papermark account — sign up at www.papermark.com/login
  2. Connect your Notion page — paste your page link (no export needed)
  3. Customize the sharing page — brand it and add a custom domain (optional)
  4. Generate a shareable link — Papermark creates a secure link
  5. Add controls (optional) — require email, set a password, or link expiry
  6. Share your link — send it to your audience
  7. Monitor activity — track views and page‑by‑page analytics
